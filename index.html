    Vegetable Pizza

    200php

    Vegetable pizza is a delightful culinary creation that features a variety of fresh vegetables as the primary toppings. Typically, it starts with a base of tomato sauce and mozzarella cheese spread over a dough crust. Then, an assortment of colorful vegetables is added on top, such as bell peppers, onions, mushrooms, tomatoes, olives, spinach, and sometimes even artichokes or zucchini. These toppings not only add vibrant colors but also a medley of flavors and textures, ranging from crunchy to tender.
